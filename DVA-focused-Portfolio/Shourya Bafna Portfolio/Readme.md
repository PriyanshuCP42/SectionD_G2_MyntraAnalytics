## Featured Projects

### 1. [AirBnB NYC Exploratory Data Analysis](https://github.com/shourya2006/AirBnB---Exploratory-Data-Analysis)

* **Tech Stack:** Tableau, EDA
* **Description:** A comprehensive analysis of 48k+ AirBnB listings in New York City. Features an interactive dashboard uncovering pricing trends, neighborhood demand, and room type distributions across the five boroughs.

### 2. [Myntra Fashion Analytics](https://github.com/PriyanshuCP42/SectionD_G2_MyntraAnalytics)

* **Tech Stack:** Python, ETL, Pandas, Statistical Analysis
* **Description:** Decoding India's online fashion market. This project involves a complex ETL pipeline and statistical analysis of 500k+ products to optimize pricing and identify margin leakage.

### 3. [Online Retail Revenue &amp; Customer Analytics](https://github.com/neo0007777/SectionD-Group12-Online_Retail)

* **Tech Stack:** Excel, RFM Analysis, Business Intelligence
* **Description:** Analyzing global transactional data to identify high-value customers through RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation and seasonal trend detection.

### 4. [FIFA World Cup Exploratory Data Analysis](https://github.com/shourya2006/FIFA-World-Cup---Exploratory-Data-Analysis)

* **Tech Stack:** Tableau, Sports Analytics
* **Description:** A visual deep dive into 80+ years of FIFA World Cup history, exploring goal trends, match outcomes, and country performance metrics.
